Invitation to 2025 WCKSS (Jeju) - Abstract Deadline Extended to Sept 26

Dear Esteemed Scholars and Researchers in Physical Education, Sport, and Physical Activity,

On behalf of the Asian Society of Kinesiology (ASK), it is my great pleasure to warmly invite you to the 2025 World Congress on Kinesiology and Sport Science (2025 WCKSS).

❏ Event Schedule & Registration Status

This year’s congress will be held from October 25 to 27 at the Jeju International Convention Center. We are preparing an intellectually vibrant program with more than 80 distinguished speakers, including world-leading scholars with H-indices of 55-140, who will deliver keynote lectures and lead panel discussions.

With 400+ accepted abstracts and over 750 pre-registered participants, the 2025 WCKSS is poised to be one of the most dynamic and productive forums for scholarly exchange in recent years — offering exceptional opportunities for learning, networking, and collaboration

❏ Abstract Submission Deadline Extended
  - September 26

To encourage even broader participation, we have extended the abstract submission deadline to Friday, September 26. We warmly encourage you to share your latest research and contribute to the growing body of knowledge that is shaping the future of kinesiology and sport science.

❏ ASK Vision & Why This Congress Matters

ASK is committed to fostering global collaboration by rotating our congress annually across Asia. The 2025 WCKSS in Jeju, Korea will go beyond sharing cutting-edge research — it will be a historic forum for exploring the worldwide development of physical education and sport science, bridging disciplinary terminology across continents, and seeking philosophical consensus on our shared research goals. This is a rare opportunity to:

  • Engage with some of the most respected thought leaders in the field.
  • Build meaningful international collaborations.
  • Contribute to a significant moment in the history of kinesiology and sport science.

We sincerely invite you to be part of this inspiring and transformative event. Your participation and support will help shape the direction of our field for years to come.

Thank you, and we look forward to welcoming you to Jeju this October.

September 2025
